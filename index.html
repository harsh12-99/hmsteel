2. Pre cast pannel welding

•   A "precast connection weld" refers to the welding process used in connecting precast concrete elements or components together. Precast concrete is a construction technique where concrete elements, such as beams, columns, panels, and slabs, are cast in a controlled environment (typically a factory) before being transported to a construction site and assembled to create a building or structure.
